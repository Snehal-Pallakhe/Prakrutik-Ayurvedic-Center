Nadi Pariksha

Nadi Pariksha is the ancient ayurvedic technique of diagnosis through the pulse. It can accurately diagnose physical, mental and emotional imbalances as well as diseases. It is a non- invasive science that enables to reach the root cause of health issues and not just address the symptoms.We will create a bespoke weight loss diet plan to help you achieve your goals. We need to understand your health status, medical history, food preferences and previous weight loss attempts, to help us shape the right diet plan for you. The way you metabolise food, and break down food into key nutrients is personal to you. Many different factors affect how you metabolise food, including your gender, age, health and stress. Your gut health is also hugely important, and some functional tests can give us clues about how food is metabolised in your body, and if you are struggling with malnutrition of certain key vitamins and minerals.
